Domain Introduction

Smart city planning involves the integration of technology and data-driven strategies to improve the quality of life in urban areas. This includes the development of intelligent infrastructure, sustainable practices, and efficient services. The primary goal is to create a more livable, workable, and sustainable urban environment through the use of smart technologies and innovative approaches [1].

Market Analysis

The global smart city market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing adoption of smart technologies in urban development. According to recent reports, the market is expected to reach USD 2.57 trillion by 2025, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 18.4% from 2020 to 2025 [2]. Key areas of investment include smart infrastructure, smart governance, and smart mobility, among others [3].
