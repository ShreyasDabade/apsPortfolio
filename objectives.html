Objectives

To promote citizen participation and governance through IT and ICT services.
To encourage a mixed-use built environment with public spaces and housing for all.
To provide education and healthcare opportunities for all citizens, ensuring safety.
To promote the city's unique identity and culture, fostering a strong economy.
To reduce pollution, increase renewable energy use, and implement energy-efficient practices.
To provide reliable transportation and mobility options, prioritizing walkability and accessibility.
To ensure reliable water supply and advanced waste management systems.
