Objectives

  • To promote citizen participation and governance through robust IT connectivity and ICT-enabled government services.
  • To ensure a mixed-use built environment that encourages compactness and provides sufficient public open spaces and housing for all income groups.
  • To offer education and healthcare opportunities for all citizens, ensuring safety and security for everyone.
  • To celebrate and promote the unique identity and culture of the city while fostering a robust and resilient economic base.
  • To maintain reduced pollution levels, increase the use of renewable energy sources, and implement energy-efficient practices.
  • To provide reliable transportation and mobility options that prioritize walkability and accessibility for all.
  • To ensure a reliable water supply and advanced water management, along with effective waste and waste water management systems.
